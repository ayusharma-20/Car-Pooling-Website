Carbon Credits

Carbon Credits are like tokens. It's a reward system designed to give back to our users for choosing carpooling. Everytime you travel using PoolPal your reward is generated on the basis of your contribution towards the environment that would be impossible otherwise, if you were to use your personal vehicles.

These tokens can be exchanged for merchandise, travel and shopping vouchers.

For Every Kilometer Travelled, our users will be getting 5 Carbon Credits. You can enjoy cheaper travel and get Rewarded at the same time. These tokens can also be redeemed to compensate for a chunk of your travel fare.
